Que: Spring IoC (Inversion of Control)

Ans.

IoC is a concept where Spring takes care of creating objects and connecting them instead of you doing it manually.

Why IoC is Useful:
Makes code cleaner and easier to test.
Reduces dependencies between parts of your app.
